Introduction

This Sensor Lab platform enables students and researchers to conduct real-time AI-integrated sensor experiments. It’s designed around Scientech’s modular equipment to measure and interpret physical parameters like light, temperature, and motion.

Components

  • Light Sensors: LDR, Photodiode, Phototransistor
  • Temperature Sensors: RTD, Thermocouple
  • Motion Sensors: Accelerometer, Gyroscope
  • Signal Conditioning: Op-Amps, Amplifiers
  • Data Interface: DAC, ADC, Digital Output

Working Principle (Click to Expand)

  • Data Collection: Sensors gather real-world data in real time.
  • AI Processing: Embedded algorithms analyze input data.
  • Feedback Loop: Adjustments are made based on AI insights for automation.

Lab Experiments (Click to Expand)

  • Experiment 1: Temperature control using thermocouple
  • Experiment 2: LDR-based light detection and response system
  • Experiment 3: Photodiode linearity and characteristics
  • Experiment 4: Pressure or vibration monitoring with AI feedback

Applications

  • Temperature control in chemical reactions
  • Light-sensitive automation for smart devices
  • Predictive AI modeling in experimental setups

Benefits

  • Improved accuracy in data acquisition
  • AI-driven decision making and predictive analysis
  • Modular learning: Sensor theory + practical implementation

Future Scope

  • Integration with IoT devices and cloud data logging
  • Advanced AI models for anomaly detection
  • Cross-domain applications in healthcare, robotics, and environmental sensing
